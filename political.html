Political Committee

Issues:

Issue 1: The Military Occupation of the Crimean Penninsula

Issue 2: The Commercialisation and Militarisation of Space


A message from your chair:

Welcome to what is (in my utterly unbiased opinion) the best committee at ALTYMUN 2022!

This is a committee for delegates passionate about global political affairs who are going to engage in sophisticated and fruitful debate over both issues, while also - of course - having lots of fun. We are going to be debating two issues which have become even more prevelant in mainstream media after my choosing of them. The issue of the militarisation of the Crimean peninsula has reached global headlines with the recent aggression from Russian troops on the Ukrainian border. Meanwhile, the amount of funding being used by governments and private investors in space exploration is being called into question, particularly in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic. As we stand on potentially the brink of European conflict, the idea of satellites being used in warfare is terrifying, and comes with profound international consequences.

I am excited for a stimulating day of debate and look forward to welcoming all delegates to this committee!

Make sure to send your resolutions to 17EMcgurk@aggs.bfet.uk by 23:59 on March 4th
