Important Information

How do I write a resolution?

You can find some excellent tips on how to write a resoultion here, and don't be afraid to ask some of the more experienced delegates in your school or committee to have a look over what you have written.

Can I apply to guest chair?

Guest chair applications for ALTYMUN2022 have now closed. If you have applied and have been selected to chair, we will contact you with more information, and to tell you as such, as soon as we can.

Which Schools Are Coming?

We have plenty of schools coming to our conference: AGGS, Stockport Grammar, Manchester Grammar, Manchester High, Withington, Cheadle Hulme School, Wilmslow High, Queens School Chester, and AGSB

Will catering be provided?

There will be light refreshments, as well as a tuck shop for our charity, and thankfully we will be able to open the canteen to provide lunch.

What should I wear?

The dress code for MUN is formal dress: a suit and tie, or a blazer and a skirt would be appropriate.

Why should I come?

Because MUN is a great chance not only to dicuss geopolical issues, but also to have fun and make friends!
