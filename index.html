Important Information

How do I write a resolution?

You can find an example resolution here, and have a look over this document for a full resolution writing guide.

Can I apply to guest chair?

Yes! We would love to have you apply. If you are interested in being a guest chair at ALTYMUN, fill out this word document and send it to alytmun@gmail.com before 4th February.

Which Schools Are Coming?

We have lots of schools coming to our conference: AGGS, SGS, MGS, Manchester high, Withington, Cheadle, Loretto Grammar, Loretto College, Saint Ambrose, and AGSB

Will catering be provided?

There will be light refreshments, as well as a tuck shop for our charity, and thankfully we will be able to open the canteen to provide lunch.

What should I wear?

The dress code for MUN is formal dress: a suit and tie, or a blazer and a skirt would be appropriate.

Why should I come?

Because MUN is a great chance not only to dicuss geopolical issues, but also to have fun and make friends!
