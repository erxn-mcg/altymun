A message from your chair:
Welcome to the 2022 Altymun DISEC committee!
This year the DISEC committee is focused on addressing issues that have emerged with 4th generation warfare, as the landscape of modern warfare and hence modern security measures is continuously changing. Biosecurity and Weaponisation of AI are prevelent modern issues were technological advancements means that the progress of which these advance outstrips the process of defining regulations and guidelines. We are very glad to have all of you in the DISEC committee and look forward to seeing you on the day for fun and fruitful debate! :)
